My Hobby: Reading

Dive into the world of books — imagination, knowledge, and adventure waiting on every page.

What Is Reading?

Reading is an immersive activity that allows me to explore new worlds, understand different cultures, and expand my knowledge on various subjects. Whether it's a thrilling mystery, a heartfelt romance, or a thought-provoking non-fiction book, reading offers a rich escape from reality and challenges my mind.

Why I Enjoy Reading

Reading provides a mental retreat. It's not just about escaping into fictional worlds, but also about discovering different perspectives. It deepens my understanding of human experiences, challenges my views, and has become a source of relaxation and personal growth. Reading also improves focus, which is essential in this fast-paced world.

My Reading Journey

I've been reading since I was a child, but my passion for books really took off in my teenage years. Over the past few years, I have made reading a consistent part of my life. In 2024, I set myself a challenge to read 50 books, and I successfully reached that goal! Some of my favorite genres include fiction, self-development, and historical novels.

Reading Tips

Start with short books or audiobooks to build a habit. Keep a reading journal to document thoughts on each book. If you’re busy, try reading during commute times or listening to audiobooks when exercising!

Fun Fact

The longest novel ever written is “In Search of Lost Time” by Marcel Proust, with a word count exceeding 1.2 million words! It would take months to finish if you read one chapter a day!
